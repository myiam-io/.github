**MyIAM** is an Identity & Access Management platform that handles
sign-up, login, and user management — so you can focus on your product.

Integrate step by step using OAuth2 / OIDC.

### Features

- **Social Login** — Kakao, Naver, Google, Apple with one click
- **Passkeys** — Passwordless auth with FIDO2 / WebAuthn
- **Email Login** — Simple login with Magic Code
- **User Management** — Centralized member platform with privacy controls
- **Policy & Terms** — Consent tracking and policy management
- **Compliance** — Audit logs, encryption, and ISMS-P support
